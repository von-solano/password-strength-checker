How To Create A Strong Password?

include the following...

  • 12+ Characters Longer passwords are harder to crack through brute-force or dictionary attacks!
  • Uppercase Letters Including uppercase letters increases password complexity!
  • Lowercase Letters Combining uppercase and lowercase letters increases password strength!
  • Special Characters Symbols like @, #, % make decreases predictability of passwords!
  • Numbers Mixing numbers and letters increases password security!
  • NO Common Passwords! Do NOT use common passwords such as "Password123" or "qwerty" etc...
