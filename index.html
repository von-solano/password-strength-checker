How To Create A Strong Password?

include the following...

  • 12+ Characters Short passwords can be cracked in seconds by brute-force attacks.
    Use at least 12 characters to resist most attack attempts.
  • Uppercase Letters Including uppercase letters makes passwords harder to guess.
  • Lowercase Letters Combining uppercase and lowercase letters increases password strength.
  • Special Characters Symbols like @, #, % disrupts common patterns and defend against brute-force attacks.
  • Numbers Mixing numbers and letters increases password security complexity and security.
  • NO Common Passwords! Do NOT use common passwords such as "Password123" or "qwerty" etc...
    Common passwords are often the first tried in hacking attempts.
